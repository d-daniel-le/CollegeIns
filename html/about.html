What is CollegeIns?

CollegeIns is an educational tool to help prospective future/ present college students explore certain programs, transfer opportunities, success rates, and overall school resources in one place. Searching for colleges can be overwhelming and time consuming at times. This is where CollegeIns provides clear resources and accessible data that helps any student make informed and confident educational decisions.

Who Uses CollegeIns?

College Administrators

Collegs can showcase their strengths, programs, and analytics with confidence and transparency. With CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc..), schools can present overall accurate, and up to date information to build trust bonds with future aspiring students.

Prospective Students

Students can read reviews, compare schools, view important analytics, monitor several colleges, and access available support services; all in one platform, and on any device!

Accessibility

CollegeIns is designed to uphold accessibility to the highest standard for all students. All content is designed to be screen-reader friendly, includes alternative text, uses clear and concise headings, supports overall keyboard navigation, and meets color contrast guidelines as well as ADA compliance. Typography always stays readable with 12px body text and 16px headings, aswell with fonts like Roboto, or Lato.

Style Guide

  • Font Sizes: 12px paragraphs, 16px headings
  • Fonts: Roboto, Lato
  • Primary Color: Blue / #003D79
  • Secondary Colors: #C4122F, #AAB720, #E36F1E, #F2C01E, #00AEEF, #162F57
  • Layout: Grid + Flexbox
  • Links: No decorations, custom hover colors

Project Scope

This prototype includes the home page, dashboard, user account features, summary reports, analytics, and resource pages. Live data, mobile versions, and additional functions will be completed in future updates.

Our Mission

CollegeIns aims to make college exploration transparent, accessible, and overall stress free. Our goal is to inspire students with clear data integration and insights, helping them choose the best educational path for their future.
